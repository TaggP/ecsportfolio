    How to Play

    I play girls lacrosse, which is a little different than boys. We can't hit each other's sticks, only making contact with our hands or body. We also have a less deep stick pocket, making it harder to catch and shoot. But the basic rules are;
    • Equipment: Lacrosse Goggles, Mouthguard, Stick
    • There is a 12 meter, 8 meter (defenders can only be in here for 3 seconds if they are not on a girl, and crease (Goalie circle that nobody can go into).
    • There are 3 sections of the field. Attack, Midfield, and Defensive zones. There are 12 players on the field at a time. Attack can't go into Defense, Defense can't go into attack. Midfielders can go anywhere, anyone can go into the midfield
    • No contact with your stick, only your hands and body
    • 12-inch invisible "sphere" around each girl's head that nobody can put their stick in
    • If there is a foul, girls can shoot from the 8 meters
